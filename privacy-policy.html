Welcome to TennesseeFeeds.com. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services. 2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily share with us when you interact with TennesseeFeeds.com. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Full names, email addresses, usernames, or any other identifiable details you provide when commenting, registering, or contacting us.
  • Comments and Content: Text, images, links, or other materials you submit through comments, posts, reactions, or other interactive features.
  • Preferences: Insights into your interests and preferences derived from your engagement with articles, topics, or features on the site.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use automated tools to gather data when you visit our website, including:

  • Usage Data: Your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referral URLs, pages visited, timestamps, and duration of visits.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Data collected via cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, and similar tools to track navigation patterns and enhance user experience.
  • Device Information: Details about your device, such as hardware model, screen resolution, unique device identifiers, and network information.

We leverage your information to enhance and sustain TennesseeFeeds.com, including:

  • Delivering, operating, and maintaining website functionality and services.
  • Tailoring content, layouts, and recommendations to personalize your experience.
  • Analyzing user behavior and trends to refine our content offerings and site performance.
  • Sending updates, newsletters, promotional materials, or responses to your inquiries.
  • Monetizing data through targeted advertising, partnerships, data analytics, and marketing initiatives.
  • Developing innovative features, services, or business opportunities based on aggregated insights.
  • Ensuring compliance with our terms of service and protecting against misuse or fraud.
