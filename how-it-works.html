What is TennesseeFeeds.com?

TennesseeFeeds.com is Tennessee’s premier news aggregation platform, curating the latest stories from across the Volunteer State. Whether it’s local events, politics, or community updates, our site lets you engage with news anonymously or as an authenticated user, shaping discussions with likes, comments, and votes.

How It Works

Step 1: Browse Anonymously

Dive into Tennessee news without signing up. Read articles, view comments, and explore trending stories as an anonymous user—no account needed.

Step 2: Engage Freely

Interact with news your way. Like or dislike articles, comment on stories, share posts, and upvote or downvote comments to highlight the best discussions, all while staying anonymous.

Step 3: Authenticate & Earn Points

Create a unique username to become authenticated. Earn points for every like, dislike, comment, share, upvote, or downvote. Use points to unlock badges, boost visibility, or access exclusive features.

Why Become Authenticated?

  • Earn Points: Get points for every action—liking, disliking, commenting, sharing, or voting. Points add up to showcase your influence.
  • Unlock Rewards: Redeem points for badges, profile customization, or priority in comment sections.
  • Stay Flexible: Authenticated users can still comment anonymously if they choose, blending privacy with perks.
  • Shape the News: Your votes and comments help surface the most relevant stories and discussions for Tennessee.
